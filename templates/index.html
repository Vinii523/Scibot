Sci-Bot

SCIBOT

The project “SciBot” mainly focus on to develop a chat bot that serves as a resource for the Science Students as well as other students seeking assistance with Science Formula and Numerical Problem solving. SciBot based on the Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning Techniques to provide an accurate answer, explanations, and guidance in various scientific disciplines. The project aims to create an accessible and dependable tool for science learners, educators, and enthusiasts.
